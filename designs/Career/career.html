Frequently Asked Questions

Are internships available year-round? expand_more
Yes, we have rolling intakes for our internship programs. We specifically look for students in polymer science, mechanical engineering, and industrial design during summer and winter breaks.
How does Taigus prioritize workplace safety? expand_more
Safety is our "North Star." Every employee undergoes mandatory safety training, and we maintain high-grade PPE and automated safety interlocks on all industrial equipment to minimize risk.
What are your ethical sourcing practices? expand_more
We audit our suppliers annually to ensure compliance with our ethical sourcing manifesto, focusing on fair labor practices and environmentally conscious material extraction.
