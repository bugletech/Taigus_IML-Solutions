### Industry Overview Section

The Food & Dairy industry demands packaging that performs under intense scrutiny. Key requirements include:

- Hygiene and Safety: Packaging must be non toxic and prevent any chance of contamination.
    
- Shelf Life: The container must provide a secure, often airtight seal to maintain freshness and extend product shelf life.
    
- Logistical Durability: Packaging must withstand cold storage, refrigeration, and the rigors of transport without leakage or breakage.
    

IML containers solve these challenges by offering secure, leak proof seals and labels that are fused into the plastic during manufacturing. 

This eliminates the risk of paper labels peeling or adhesive contamination, resulting in hygienic, premium food packaging that builds consumer trust.

### How to Choose the Right Container for Food & Dairy

The best packaging maximizes product protection and brand visibility. Use this food packaging selection guide:

1. Product State: For semi-liquids (curd, yogurt, lassi), prioritize secure, leak proof Snap Fit containers. For ghee or spreads, consider a Twist Pack with sealing wads.
    
2. Storage Needs: For deep freezing or cold chain, ensure the container is freezer-safe and maintains seal integrity.
    
3. Branding Goal: Choose shapes and sizes that provide maximum surface area for impactful IML graphics.
