### 2. Introduction / Industry Overview

AT Taigus, We offer premium IML ice cream containers engineered to meet the strict demands of the ice cream industry where food safety, hygiene, freezer performance, and eye-catching shelf appeal are critical.

Our snap fit containers (round, square, and oval) provide secure sealing, excellent stackability, and durable In Mould Labelling that stays perfect even in deep freeze conditions. Whether you produce premium scooping ice cream, family packs, or single serve cups, we deliver packaging that protects product quality while enhancing brand visibility.

  

### 3. Why IML Containers Are Ideal for Ice Cream

- Freezer Performance: Maintains structural integrity and lid seal even at -18°C and below
    
- Tamper Evident Safety: Builds consumer trust with secure snap fit closure
    
- Superior Branding: Permanent, high resolution IML graphics that resist condensation and scratching
    
- Leak Proof Design: Prevents melting ice cream from leaking during transport and retail display
    
- Stackable & Space Efficient: Optimizes cold storage and logistics
    
- Hygienic & Food Safe: Virgin food grade PP with no risk of label contamination
