Industries Overview

Different industries demand different packaging performance standards. Ice cream packaging requires excellent cold resistance and leak-proof sealing, while nutraceutical packaging focuses on moisture protection and product integrity.

Taigus understands these unique needs.

Our industry-specific IML containers balance safety, durability, branding power, and regulatory compliance. Using advanced In-Mould Labelling technology, we create packaging that maintains consistent high-quality branding while adapting structural and material properties to suit each application.
