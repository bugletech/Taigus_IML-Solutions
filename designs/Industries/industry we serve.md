## How to Choose the Right Industry Solution

Choosing the right packaging solution starts with understanding your product and its specific requirements. Follow this simple step by step process:

Step 1: Identify Your Product Type

Determine whether your product is frozen (ice cream), semi liquid (curd/shrikhand/lassi), oily (ghee/peanut butter), powder based (protein powder), or dry (dates/confectionery).

Step 2: Consider Storage & Distribution Needs

- Deep freezing or cold chain : freezer safe snap fit containers (Round/Oval).
    
- Moisture protection : Choose containers with excellent sealing (Twist Pack recommended for powders).
    
- Bulk or takeaway : Look for larger capacities with handles (RC-1000 / RC-2000).
    

Step 3: Evaluate Branding & Shelf Appeal

- Premium/gift packaging : Oval or Square shapes for an elegant look.
    
- High volume retail : Round Snap Fit for maximum capacity and stackability.
    

Step 4: Match with Recommended Containers

|Ghee & Peanut Butter|Twist Pack with Sealing Wads|SRT-500, SRT-1000|
|Protein Powder & Supplements|Round Snap Fit + Twist Pack|RC-500, SRT-500, SRT-1000|
