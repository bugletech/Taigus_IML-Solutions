Not Sure Which Container Is Best for Your Product?

Our packaging specialists have developed a quick logic guide to help you match your product to the perfect vessel.

1

Ice Cream & Frozen Desserts

Recommend: **Round (RC)** or **Oval (OC)** for easy scooping.

2

Nutritional Powders

Recommend: **Twist Pack (SRT)** for airtight re-sealing.

3

Retail Cookies & Snacks

Recommend: **Square (ST)** for better shelf stacking.
