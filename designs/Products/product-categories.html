Frequently Asked Questions

What is the advantage of IML over traditional labelling?

expand_more

IML labels are applied during the moulding process, making them a permanent part of the container. They resist scratching, moisture, and fading — delivering vibrant 360° branding that lasts the product's full shelf life.

Are all containers food grade and freezer safe?

expand_more

Yes. All Taigus containers are manufactured from virgin food grade polypropylene (BPA free), certified under ISO 22000:2018 and GMP standards. They are fully freezer safe and stackable.

Do you provide sealing wads and handles?

expand_more

Yes. SRT Twist Pack containers come with sealing wads for added freshness and tamper evidence. Optional handles are available on larger RC Series sizes (RC-1000 and RC-2000).

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

expand_more

For standard stock items, we support flexible MOQs. For custom-printed IML containers, the MOQ typically starts from 10,000 units per design.

Are the containers freezer and microwave safe?

expand_more

Yes, our virgin PP containers are designed to be freezer-safe. They are also suitable for microwave reheating (remove lid before microwaving).

How long does custom IML development take?

expand_more

Initial custom IML development, including design approval and plate-making, generally takes 3-4 weeks. Subsequent orders have much shorter lead times.
