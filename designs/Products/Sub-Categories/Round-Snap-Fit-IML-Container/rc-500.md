### 2. Product Overview

The RC-500 is a high performance 500ml round snap fit container designed for the dairy and food industry. Manufactured from virgin food grade polypropylene, it offers excellent durability, leak resistance, and product protection while keeping contents fresh.

Equipped with premium In Mould Labelling (IML), this container delivers seamless, high resolution graphics that enhance brand visibility and give a premium look on retail shelves and in freezers. Its lightweight yet robust design and secure snap fit lid make it perfect for both retail packs and bulk distribution.

  

### 3. Key Features & Benefits

- Premium IML Decoration: Scratch resistant, moisture proof 360° branding that never peels or fades
    
- Food Grade Safety: Made from 100% virgin BPA free polypropylene
    
- Secure Snap Fit Lid: Tamper evident, leak resistant closure for extended freshness
    
- Excellent Stackability: Saves space during storage and transportation
    
- Temperature Resistant: Suitable for freezer, refrigeration, and ambient conditions
    
- Lightweight & Durable: Easy to handle while maintaining strength
