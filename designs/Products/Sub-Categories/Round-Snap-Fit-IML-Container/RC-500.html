The Precision of Protection

The RC-500 represents the pinnacle of our Artisanal Archive. Engineered for the demanding dairy and food industry, this container is crafted from 100% virgin polypropylene, ensuring absolute safety and compliance with global food standards. Our high-resolution In-Mold Labeling (IML) technology fuses the label with the container itself, creating a seamless, waterproof, and scratch-resistant finish that elevates your brand from a commodity to a premium lifestyle choice.
