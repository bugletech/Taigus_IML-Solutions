Round vs. Oval: Choosing Your Fit

Why choose Round over Oval for logistics? expand_more

Round containers provide maximum structural integrity for vertical stacking. Their uniform shape prevents "weak spots" that can occur in oval or rectangular containers, making them ideal for long-distance cold-chain logistics.

Does the shape affect the IML application? expand_more

Round containers allow for a seamless 360-degree wrap. While oval labels require precise positioning to match the curves, round containers provide a continuous surface for vibrant, panoramic branding that looks stunning from any angle on the shelf.

Logistics Note

Consider round variants for maximum durability during rough freight. The absence of corners significantly reduces the risk of impact-induced cracking.

Frequently Asked

What is the lead time for custom IML containers?

Typically, initial custom orders take 4-6 weeks for design approval, plate making, and production. Re-orders are significantly faster.

Are these containers microwave safe?

Yes, our food-grade PP material is designed to be microwave safe for re-heating purposes (standard home microwave settings).

Can the containers be recycled?

Absolutely. Both the container and the IML label are made of Polypropylene (PP #5), making the entire package 100% recyclable in the same stream.
