What Makes Snap Fit IML Containers the Smart Choice?

The snap fit mechanism works through a precisely engineered lip and flange system. When the lid is pressed down, it locks with an audible click, creating a secure, tamper-evident seal. This design is especially valued in the food and dairy industry for preventing leaks, maintaining freshness, and giving consumers confidence.

At Taigus, we integrate this proven closure with advanced In Mould Labelling (IML). The label becomes part of the container wall during moulding resulting in a seamless, high-resolution finish that resists moisture, scratches, and condensation perfect for freezer and refrigerated display.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between snap fit and twist pack containers?

expand_more

Snap fit offers faster, one press opening with excellent tamper evidence. Twist pack provides extra sealing wads and is preferred for powders and oily products like ghee and peanut butter.

Are snap fit containers leak-proof and freezer-safe?

expand_more

Yes. The precise snap fit mechanism creates a tight seal that prevents leakage even with semi-liquid products. They maintain performance in deep freezers (-18°C and below).

Is the IML label safe for direct food contact?

expand_more

Absolutely. The label is fused into the plastic during moulding, so there is no ink migration or adhesive risk. All materials are virgin food grade PP.

What capacities are available in snap fit IML containers?

expand_more

125 ml to 2000 ml across round, square, and oval shapes.

Do you offer custom colours and branding?

expand_more
