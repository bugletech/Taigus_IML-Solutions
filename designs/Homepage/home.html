Trusted IML Container Manufacturer for Food & Dairy Brands

Originating from the industrial heart of Ahmedabad, Taigus brings a new standard of precision to the Indian packaging landscape. Our facility is equipped with state-of-the-art Japanese JSW injection moulding technology and high-speed European robotics.
