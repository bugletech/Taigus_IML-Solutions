Key Growth Drivers

Premiumisation & Consumer Trust

Rising disposable incomes and urbanisation are driving consumers toward premium packaging formats. IML's permanent, high-resolution branding creates a "no-label" look that commands shelf presence and signals quality.

Regulatory Compliance (FSSAI 2026)

Incoming FSSAI food safety and packaging amendments demand stricter migration testing and "Food Grade" certifications. IML's label-fused construction eliminates adhesive migration risk, ensuring long-term regulatory compliance.

Quick Commerce & Cold Chain Expansion

The rise of 10–30 minute grocery delivery demands packaging that survives freezer-to-doorstep transitions. Snap-fit IML containers are the structural solution — leak-proof, stackable, and thermally stable.

The IML Process
1

Label Preparation

Pre-printed IML label inserted into the mould cavity with precision robotic placement and electrostatic holding.

2

Injection Moulding

Molten virgin food-grade PP injected via Japanese JSW machines at pharma-level precision and cycle speed.

3

Label Fusion

Hot resin fuses permanently with the label — single-piece container, no adhesive, no peeling risk, ever.

4

Quality Inspection & Dispatch

Automated QC checks every container for dimensional accuracy, seal integrity and label fusion before dispatch.
