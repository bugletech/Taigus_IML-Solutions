How We Make It

The IML Manufacturing Process

Four precision steps, executed with Japanese machinery and robotic automation, every single cycle.

1

Label Preparation

Pre-printed IML labels are inspected and staged. Robotic arms place them into the mould cavity with electrostatic holding for perfect alignment.

2

Injection Moulding

Molten virgin food grade PP is injected using JSW machines at pharma level precision and cycle speed; consistent wall thickness every time.

3

Label Fusion

Hot resin fuses permanently with the label, creating a seamless single-piece container. No adhesive; no peeling risk Ever.

4

QC & Dispatch

Automated vision inspection checks every container for dimensional accuracy, seal integrity, and label fusion quality before packing and dispatch.

Talk to Our Team
What is In Mould Labelling (IML)?
IML is a process where a preprinted label is placed inside the mould before plastic is injected. The label fuses with the container as one seamless piece making it scratch-proof, waterproof, and permanent. No adhesive, no peeling, no separate labelling step.
What are your minimum order quantities (MOQ)?
We offer flexible MOQs tailored to your brand's scale from trial runs for startups to high-volume enterprise orders. Contact our sales team to discuss your specific requirements and get a customised quote.
Are your containers food grade and safe?
Yes. All Taigus containers are made from 100% virgin food-grade polypropylene (PP) completely BPA-free. We are ISO 9001:2015 certified, GMP compliant, EPR certified, and FSSAI aligned. Our pharma background founders built food safety into the DNA of our operations.
Can I customise the IML design/artwork?
Absolutely. We offer full customisation : custom IML artwork, Pantone colour matching, private label manufacturing, and in some cases modified container shapes. Our in-house printing unit ensures colour accuracy and faster turnaround from artwork to finished container.
Do your containers work in freezer conditions?
Yes. Our containers maintain structural integrity and seal performance at -30°C and are designed for India's cold chain logistics requirements. They are used extensively by ice cream and frozen dessert brands across the country.
Where is Taigus located and do you ship pan India?
Our manufacturing facility is at Plot No. 233, Bhayla-Sakodara Road, Bavla, Ahmedabad, 382240. We supply to brands across India and serve global markets. Get in touch to discuss your logistics requirements.
