The IML Process
1

Label Preparation

Pre-printed IML label inserted into the mould cavity with precision robotic placement and electrostatic holding.

2

Injection Moulding

Molten virgin food-grade PP injected using Japanese JSW machines at pharma-level precision and cycle speed.

3

Label Fusion

Hot resin fuses permanently with the label — creating a seamless, single-piece container. No adhesive, no peeling risk.

4

Quality Inspection & Dispatch

Automated QC checks every container for dimensional accuracy, seal integrity, and label fusion quality before dispatch.
