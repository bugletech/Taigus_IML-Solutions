Quality Assurance FAQ

How do you verify certifications for specific batches? expand_more
Every production run is assigned a unique batch ID linked to a digital Certificate of Analysis (CoA). This CoA confirms that the specific batch has passed all ISO 22000 and internal quality benchmarks before dispatch.
What impact does your packaging have on dairy shelf life? expand_more
Our IML containers offer superior oxygen and moisture barrier properties compared to standard labeling. This protects dairy products from oxidation and light, effectively maintaining flavor profile and extending freshness.
How is quality maintained across bulk annual orders? expand_more
We utilize precision molds that undergo scheduled sonic cleaning and inspection every 100,000 cycles. Combined with our automated vision systems, this prevents any "mold-drift" or quality degradation over large volumes.
Can we request specific compliance documentation? expand_more
Yes. We provide full documentation packages including Migration Test Reports, Food Grade Declarations, and ISO Audit summaries upon request to support your brand's regulatory requirements.
