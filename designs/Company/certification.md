## Ensuring Food Safety and Hygiene

For our clients in the food and dairy sectors, safety is non-negotiable. Our strict adherence to food grade standards is validated through rigorous third party audits.

- Food Grade Materials: We exclusively use virgin, certified food contact safe plastic raw materials.
    
- Hygienic Manufacturing: Our facilities follow stringent hygiene protocols to minimize contamination risks during the IML process.
    
- Traceability: A robust system ensures complete material traceability from source to finished container.
    

These measures give you complete confidence that every Taigus IML container meets the highest food safety requirements.

## Commitment to Audits and Compliance Updates

At Taigus, compliance is an ongoing journey, not a one time achievement.

1. Annual Audits: We proactively schedule and undergo regular audits for ISO 22000,EPR,  GMP, and other relevant certifications.
