About Taigus | Excellence in IML Packaging

We are an emerging leader in premium In Mould Labelling (IML) food containers, committed to delivering superior quality, innovative design, and reliable supply to brands across India.

From our modern facility in Bavla near Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India, we combine advanced technology with a deep understanding of food packaging needs to help brands enhance product safety, shelf appeal, and consumer trust.

Foundations

Our Story

Taigus Pvt. Ltd. was founded in 2022 with a clear vision: to revolutionize rigid plastic packaging through high quality IML technology.

Born in the industrial heartland of Ahmedabad, Gujarat, we started with a simple goal to offer food-grade containers that combine exceptional functionality with stunning visual appeal. Today, we have grown into a trusted name known for precision manufacturing, consistent quality, and customer centric solutions.

Our journey is driven by the belief that great packaging does more than protect; it elevates the brand and delights the consumer.

Who We Are

Taigus is a specialized IML (In Mould Labelling) packaging manufacturer focused on the food and dairy industry. We produce a range of snap fit and twist pack containers (125ml to 2000ml) designed for ice cream, curd, shrikhand, lassi, ghee, peanut butter, protein powder, and more.

What sets us apart is our unwavering focus on:

  • check_circle Food grade safety and hygiene
  • check_circle Premium branding through advanced IML technology
  • check_circle Reliable scalability for both growing and established brands

Headquartered in Bodakdev, Ahmedabad, with manufacturing in Bavla, we combine pharma level precision with practical food packaging expertise.
