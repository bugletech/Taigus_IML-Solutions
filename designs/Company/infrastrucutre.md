### IML Process & Workflow Section

At the heart of our capability is the In Mould Labelling (IML) process. Unlike traditional labelling methods, IML integrates the pre printed label directly into the container during the moulding process.

The label fuses permanently with the plastic, creating a seamless, highly durable finish that is resistant to moisture, scratching, and temperature variations. This integrated approach ensures superior hygiene, consistent branding quality, and long lasting visual appeal on every container we produce.
